Ratatouille

Ingredients

Pasta Sauce 1.5 cups
Zucchini 1
Squash 1
Eggplant 1
Onions 1
Pepper 1

You probably already have:

Olive Oil 1 Tablespoon
Thyme
Salt
Pepper
Majoram
Oregano

Preparation

Preheat oven to 200°C.

  1. In a medium cast-iron skillet, spread tomato sauce evenly across the bottom of the skillet.

  2. Using a sharp knife, thinly slice the zucchini, squash, eggplant, onion, and pepper into rounds.

  3. Alternating the vegetables, create small stacks. Lay the stack on top of the marinara sauce and continue the process until the skillet is filled, then sprinkle with oregano, salt, pepper, and a drizzle of olive oil.

  4. Bake for 60 minutes and serve hot over quinoa, brown rice, or even pasta!
Enjoy your meal!
