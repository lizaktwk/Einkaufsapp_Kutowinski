Tomato Soup with Basil

Ingredients

Onions 0.5
Carrots 1
Cut tomatoes 400g
Basil 5g

You probably already have:

Oil 1 Tablespoon
Garlic 0.5
Salt
Pepper
Sugar
Oregano, dried

Preparation

  1. Halve, peel and dice the onions. Peel and chop the garlic. wash carrots, peel and dice.

  2. Heat oil in a pan on high and sauté onions, garlic and carrots for about 3 minutes. Add the tomato paste and fry for another 1 minute. Stir broth if necessary and pot with chopped tomatoes and broth infuse Season with salt, pepper, sugar and oregano and simmer on medium for approx. 10 min.

  3. Wash the basil, shake dry, pluck the leaves from the stems and chop finely. Finely puree the tomato soup and taste again if necessary. Divide among plates and serve garnished with basil.
Enjoy!
