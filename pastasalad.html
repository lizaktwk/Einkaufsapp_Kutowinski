Italian Pasta Salad

Ingredients

Cucumber 0.5
Sun dried tomatoes as many as you like
Parmesan Cheese a much as you like
Arugula 20g
Farfalle 400g

You probably already have:

Olive Oil 1/4 cup
Balsamico Vinegar 3 tablespoons
Oregano 2 teaspoons
Honey 1 teaspoon
Pepper
Salt

Preparation

  1. Cook the pasta al dente in a large stockpot of generously-salted water according to package instructions. Drain pasta, then rinse under cold water for about 20-30 seconds until no longer hot. Transfer the pasta to a large mixing bowl.

  2. Add cucumber, arugula and sun dried tomatoes to the mixing bowl, then drizzle all of the vinaigrette evenly on top. Toss until all of the ingredients are evenly coated with the dressing.

  3. Serve immediately, garnished with extra feta and black pepper if desired.

  4. To make the vinaigrette: Whisk all ingredients together until combined.
Enjoy your salad!
